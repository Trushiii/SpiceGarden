Common Questions

Frequently Asked

Do you take walk-in guests?

Yes! Walk-ins are welcome, though we recommend reserving a table on weekends and public holidays to avoid wait times.

Can you cater for large groups?

Absolutely. We accommodate groups of up to 50 in our private dining room. Please contact us at least 48 hours in advance.

Do you offer takeaway or delivery?

Takeaway is available daily. Delivery is available through PickMe Food and Uber Eats within a 10 km radius of Colombo 03.

Are you allergy-friendly?

We take allergies very seriously. Please notify us of any dietary requirements when booking and we'll ensure safe, delicious options.

Do you have a kids' menu?

Yes! Our junior menu features mild, child-friendly fusion dishes crafted with the same quality ingredients as our main menu.

Can I bring my own cake?

Of course! We welcome celebration cakes with prior notice. A small corkage fee of LKR 500 applies for outside desserts.

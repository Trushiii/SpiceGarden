Our Story

Born from the Heart
of Sri Lanka

SpiceGarden was born from a simple belief — that great food is the most honest form of storytelling. Our founder, Head Chef, grew up in the spice-rich hills of Kandy, learning recipes passed down through generations.

Today, we blend those ancient traditions with modern fusion techniques, creating dishes that are bold, unexpected, and deeply rooted in the flavours of South Asia.
